Challenges

In this buzy world, people have forgotten about the hunger and poverty around them.We should time and donate food which is extra rather than feeding them to the trash

1/3rd of the world’s hungry live in India. India has 212 million undernourished people but irony is that 40% of food is wasted in India according to UN. We are sure that food is in surplus but unevenly distubuted. The main challenge here that to motivate people to donate food everytime they see it getting wasted
